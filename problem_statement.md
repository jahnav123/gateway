The current manual process for approving student permissions causes significant time wastage and disruption for faculty members. Class teachers and Heads of Departments (HODs) are frequently interrupted during lectures, research, or administrative meetings to review and sign physical permission slips. These constant, unscheduled interruptions break their workflow and reduce the overall time they can spend on high-quality teaching and departmental management.

Because there is no digital system, faculty members must also manually keep track of these paper slips, which is disorganized and prone to error. This makes it difficult for the HOD to quickly verify how many students are off-campus or to look up past records for accountability. By moving to an automated digital platform, teachers and HODs can approve requests in batches during their own designated free time, eliminating classroom interruptions and creating an instant, searchable record of all student movement.
